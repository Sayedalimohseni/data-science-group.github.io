Challenges

AI-enabled Banking

Banking across the world has undergone a critical transformation in the last decades. In the last few years, with the advancement in the technology, banking has adopted the use of new technological tools, and today, the advancement in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science has the potential to enable the banks to use the big data in an smart way and use it to automate and facilitate many data-driven processes including: Fraud Detection, Customer Segmentation, Customer Experience, Intelligent Recommendation, Personalized Banking, Real- Time and Predictive Analytics, Risk Modelling and More.

The challenges in this hackathon will focus on novel applications of AI in banking from machine learning and data analytics to process automation and cognitive assistants.
