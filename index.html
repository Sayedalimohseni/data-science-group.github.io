Prospective Students


Here are some values that we would like to see in you:

- Skills: You should have either a strong software engineering background or a strong ML/AI background.
- Hard work: We expect you to have a strong work ethic.
- Flexibility: You should be willing to dive into different facets of a project.
- Learning: You should have a strong growth mindset, and want to learn continuously.
- Teamwork: You are expected to support and collaborate with others.
