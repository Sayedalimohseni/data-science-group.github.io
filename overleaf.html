Shaing Documents with me on OverLeaf

Dear Students & Postdocs,

For the projects that you have will be sharing with me on OverLeaf, please use the following naming outline:

[YYYY]-[YourName+First3charatersOfYourFamily(Capital)]-[PublicationType]-[Venue]

For example:
- Alex Brown will name his 2019 MRes thesis as follow:
  2019-AlexBRO-MResThesis-MQ
- Sara Atkins will share her 2020 VLDB paper as:
  2020-SaraATK-Conf-VLDB

Please also note that Macquarie University is providing free Overleaf Professional accounts for students and staffs:
https://www.overleaf.com/edu/macquarie

Thanks, Amin
